# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Who We Are

Human Layer is operated by Douglas Johnson. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

## What We Collect

When you use Human Layer, we collect:

- **Account information**: Your email address and/or phone number, used to identify your account and enable sign-in.
- **Profile data**: Information you choose to provide, such as preferences and settings.
- **Usage data**: How you interact with the service, including conversations and content you create.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Authenticate you**: Verify your identity when you sign in via email magic link, phone OTP, or Google OAuth.
- **Provide the service**: Enable the core features of Human Layer.
- **Communicate with you**: Send account-related messages such as sign-in codes, security alerts, or important service updates.

We do not use your phone number or email for marketing purposes.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Human Layer:

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication infrastructure.
- **Twilio**: Phone number verification and SMS delivery for sign-in codes.
- **Google**: OAuth sign-in option.
- **Stripe**: Payment processing and billing.
- **Sentry**: Error tracking and performance monitoring.

These providers process your data according to their own privacy policies and are used solely to provide our service to you.

## What We Don't Do

- We **do not sell** your personal information.
- We **do not share** your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
- We **do not send** unsolicited marketing messages.

## Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## Your Rights

You may:

- **Access** your data through your account settings.
- **Update** your email or phone number in settings.
- **Reset** your data using the Reset Data feature in settings.
- **Delete** your account by contacting us.

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the service or via email.
